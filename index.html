Kimberley Atkinson is one of Christchurch’s most qualified Permanent Makeup Artists. she is also an Internationally Qualified Beauty Therapist and Eyelash Technician.

Growing up in rural Hawkes Bay, Kimberley was always creative. Her love for creativity flourished even more when she discovered the power and art of makeup. This is what began her journey to being a Beauty Therapist and Permanent Makeup Artist.

Since becoming an Internationally Qualified Beauty Therapist, Kimberley has trained with elite Permanent Makeup companies, including; PhiAcademy, Deluxe Brows, Feather Touch by Cherie, Monica Ivani and Suzie McIntosh Brows.

“ I believe that Permanent Makeup gives women the confidence they deserve!”

-Kimberley Atkinson
